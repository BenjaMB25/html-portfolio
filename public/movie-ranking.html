My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

Knifes Out

Imagine a mind blowing crime thriller combined with the most hillarious situations and a golden cast

Devil Wears Prada

This is is a slice of life where it is from the point of view of Andy, a rookie journalist, which starts working for Miranda Priestly, an icon in the fashion world. Throughout the movie we see how Andy adapts to the harsh enviroment of the fashion and manages to keep balanced her professional life and her personal life.
